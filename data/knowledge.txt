Unicorn

General Characteristics:
- A mythical creature resembling a white horse
- Has a single long horn on its forehead
- Beautiful white shimmering coat
- Possesses magical powers

Special Abilities:
- The unicorn's horn can heal diseases and neutralize poisons
- Runs faster than ordinary horses
- Can fly according to some legends
- Has high intelligence and wisdom

Habitat:
- Mysterious forests
- Sacred meadows
- Remote high mountains

Mythological History:
- Appears in legends across many world cultures
- Often associated with purity and magic
- Symbolizes goodness and hope
